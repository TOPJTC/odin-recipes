Beef Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 1 can refried beans
  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 can rotel
  • 1/2 onion diced
  • seasonings
  • tortillas
  • cheese

Instructions:

  1. Brown ground beef and half of the onions in a large pan over med/high heat
  2. Mix in the spices and continue cooking while stirring for 3-5 min until thoroughly coated
  3. Ad refried beans and rotel and continue stirring until heated through
  4. Spoon onto toasted tortillas with cheese and raw onion

Notes:

  • Normally I drain the fat whenever I brown ground beef but not with this dish, The beans need the fat.
  • After step 2, you have basic taco meat, good for many dishes like taco salads or added to queso.
  • Beef and bean filling is also a great bean dip for a party
  • Finally, always toast your tortillas. If you do not have a comal or a griddle, a heavy pan will do.
    Use medium heat and flip every 15-20 seconds until it takes a little color and blisters start to inflate.
