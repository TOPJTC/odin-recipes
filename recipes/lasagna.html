The Worlds Best Homemade Three Cheese Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Lasagna Ingredients

The Allrecipes community adores this lasagna recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatos
  • Sugar
  • Spices/Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

How to make - Step by Step

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the "How to Layer" below
  5. Cover with foil and bake according to the "How to Bake" below
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

How to Layer your Three Cheese Lasanga:

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta Mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat Sauce
  6. Parmesan Cheese
  7. Repeat layers and top with Parmesan Cheese

How to Bake your Three Cheese Lasanga:

  1. Preheat oven to 370 F
  2. Bake with the foil still covering the dish for 30 min.
  3. Uncover dish and continue baking for another 20-30 min.
  4. Left uncovered to rest at room temp. for approx 15 min.
