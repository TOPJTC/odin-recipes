Three Eggs and Meat

Three Eggs and Meat is just a simple take of over easy eggs, sausage links, bacon and ham slices served with breakfast potatos and your choice of toast. A simple but delious meal, thats honestly acceptable for breakfast, lunch or dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 3 slices of bacon
  • 3 slices of ham
  • 3 sausage links
  • 3 russel potatos
  • 1 onion
  • butter
  • bread
  • spices

Preperations:

  1. Start with washing potatos and slicing into cubes
  2. Dice your onion
  3. Get a pan on med/high heat
  4. Get toaster, bread and butter set up

Cooking:

  1. Starting with the meats, get all 9 pieces of meat into the pan and cook until your liking
  2. After all the meat is prepared, in the same pan you want to toss in your potatos and onions
  3. *Add butter to pan if needed*
  4. As your potatos are begining to brown start cooking your toast
  5. Once potatoes are nice and golden brown, pull off heat and season
  6. Butter toast
